## üìù NOTES FOR ARTISTS

1. **Consistent Style:** All assets must maintain paper-cutout aesthetic with visible texture
2. **Outlines:** Use thick black outlines (3-4px) for all characters and items
3. **Shadows:** Soft drop shadows beneath all characters (15% opacity, 2-3px offset)
4. **Paper Texture:** Apply subtle grain overlay to maintain craft aesthetic
5. **Fold Lines:** Add subtle crease lines to show paper layering
6. **Transparency:** All assets must have transparent backgrounds (PNG format)
7. **Color Consistency:** Stick to the defined color palette
8. **Scale:** Maintain relative sizes (Hero: 128px, Enemies: 96px, Bosses: 256px)
